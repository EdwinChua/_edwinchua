## How to Use This Calculator

1. **Take-Home Income** is set at $7,553 (your monthly income after CPF)
2. **Total Allocated Expenses** - Manually update this with the SUM total from your Budget Allocation database
3. **Available Cash** - Manually update this as: $7,553 - Total Expenses

To get the Total Allocated Expenses:

- Go to your <database url="[https://www.notion.so/364fd3f09f764f40ae3502c3a1880976">Budget](https://www.notion.so/364fd3f09f764f40ae3502c3a1880976%22%3EBudget) Allocation</database> database
- Click on the "Monthly Amount" column header
- Select "Calculate" → "Sum"
- Copy that total and update the "Total Allocated Expenses" row above
