## 🗂 Databases

- 📥 **Inbox** — capture anything quickly, process later
- 🚀 **Projects** — active work and personal projects
- 📝 **Notes** — atomic notes, ideas, and knowledge
- 📚 **Resources** — articles, books, and references
- ✈️ **Travel** — trips, destinations, and places
- 🍷 **Wine Log** — bottles tasted, ratings, flavor notes
- 🍷 **Wine Knowledge** — beginner's guide to regions and grapes
- 💼 **Work** — tasks, projects, meeting notes, and performance appraisals

---

## 🤖 AI Agent Instructions

This workspace is structured for easy querying. Each database uses consistent properties:

- **Tags** — topic labels for cross-referencing
- **Status** — current state of an item
- **Created/Updated** — timestamps for recency filtering
- **Area** — life domain (Work, Personal, Learning, Travel)

To find anything, search by tag, area, or status across databases.
