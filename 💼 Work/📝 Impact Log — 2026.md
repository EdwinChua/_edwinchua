## 🤖 How to Generate Your Appraisal

When you're ready to write your year-end assessment, paste your entries into a Claude chat and say:

> *"Based on this impact log, write a year-end self-assessment for my performance appraisal. Structure it by: key achievements, business impact, collaboration, and areas of growth. Tone should be confident but factual."*
